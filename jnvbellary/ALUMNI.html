THE ALUMNI MEET-2016-17


The Alumni Meet was organised in the Vidyalaya on 5th February 2017. The occasion was graced by honourable DC, Sri. A.Y Reddy, Dc, NVS, Hyd Region, respected AC, Shri. Gopalakrishna and Shri. Ravindranath Babu, donor of the Vidyalaya about 150-200 Alumni members attended the Meet. The initiative was taken by 6th Batch Alumni students.

Honourable Dc lauded the effects and contribution of the Alumni for the development of the Vidyalaya. Honourable AC, Shri. Gopalakrishna appreciated the Alumni of Karnataka Schools who achively participate in the Alumni meets and are concerned about the welfare of the home Vidyalayas.
