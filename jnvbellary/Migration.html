  Migration of Students  

One of the important features of the Navodaya Vidyalaya Scheme is the Scheme of exchange of students from one Navodaya Vidyalaya in a particular linguistic region to another Vidyalaya in a different linguistic region to promote understanding of the diversity and plurality of India's culture and people. According to the Scheme 30% of children from one Navodaya Vidyalaya are migrated to another Vidyalaya at Class - IX level. The migration takes place generally between Hindi and Non-Hindi speaking districts. Migration status of this vidyalaya is given below:
