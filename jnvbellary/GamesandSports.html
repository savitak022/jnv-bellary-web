ANNUAL SPORTS DAY


The Annual Sports Day was organised in our vidyalaya on 10.01.2017. shri. Savishankar Naik DYSP Kudligi, graced the occasion. The other guests of honour were Shri. Ravindranath Babu and Shri. Revanna Zilla Parishad Trophes and merit certificates were distributed. The hall mark of the Sports Day was the March Past by NCC, Scouts and Guides and respective houses. The Aravali house was declared as the sports Champion.
