THE ANNUAL DAY

The Annual Day of the vidyalaya was held with Shri. Shivananda (retd. Kannada Professor) gracing the occasion as the Chief Guest Dr. RavindranathBabu, Ex-MLA, was the guest of honour. The meritorious candidates in the academic area were awarded prizes and certificates. The festive occasion was marked by cultural bonanza of dances and skits. The Shivalik house was declared as the academic winner.
