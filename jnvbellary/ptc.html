PARENTS TEACHERS' COUNCIL

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya being Co-educational and Residential in nature the participation and support of local community and parents will give a great fillip to the scheme and would also ensure in complementing the effectiveness and utility of education imported in these institutions in order to develop co-operation between the school and parents, the Samiti has instructed the Vidyalaya to constitute well knit Parent Teacher Council for strengthening the functioning of the Vidyalaya.

The PTC constitutes elected members from parents' side, each from every class and 5 members from teaching staff. The Chairman of the PTC is Principal.

The following are the aims and objective of the PTC:

•  To maintain continuous interaction between parents and teachers/school authorities.

•  To involve the parents in the development of the Vidyalaya by utilizing their experiences and expertise.

•  To assist the Vidyalaya in resource mobilization and development of Infrastructure facilities and their maintenance.

•  To assist the Chairman VMC in taking fast and effective decisions.

•  To popularize the advantages of residential culture in terms of Academic excellence character building and excellence.

•  To liaise with various organization engaged in community service programme through participatory activities of  

    children, parents and teachers, and

•  To develop network of Alumni and their parents to help the institution to play its role effectively as a pace setup.
