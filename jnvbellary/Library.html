Library


ABOUT OUR SCHOOL LIBRARY


Library is a part of an educational institution. It plays an important role in the field of development and career. building of students and teachers.

Our school library has 9168 books, 25 magazines and 23 newspapers as on date.


OUR LIBRARY ACTIVITIES:

1.Book Review: Preparation of wall magazines, scrap book preparation.

2.National Book: Every year we celebrate NATIONAL BOOK WEEK from 14th Nov to 20th Nov of the year 2016. On this occasion, we conducted so many competitions, guest lectures. Some of the competitions which were conducted in the BOOK WEEK are :

1.On 14/11/2016, Smt. Maniben, Vice Principal of the Vidyalaya, staff and students joined their hands to inaugurate the ‘BOOK WEEK’ function.
2. On 15/11/2016, Cross Word Puzzle was conducted in Vidyalaya and students actively participated in the competition.
3. On 16/11/2016, Pick and Speak competition was conducted, from each house every student talked about different topics.
4. On 17/11/2016, Book Review Committee conducted ‘BOOK REVIEW COMPETITION’.
5. On 18/11/2016, Memory contest about book and author was conducted. The students actively participated in the competition.
6. On 19/11/2016, Debate Competition was conducted on the topic ‘BOOKS v/s ELECTRONIC MEDIA’. The competition was successfully conducted.
7. On 20/11/2016, Valedictory Programme was held and winners for the conducted programmes were awarded prizes.
