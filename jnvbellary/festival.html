Festival


GANIT SAPTAH – 2016-17

Date- 21/12/2016

“GanitSaptah” was organised in morning assembly by Mathematics club. It was inaugurated by the Principal ,ShriA.Sundar. Fascinating facts in maths where explained by Mr. M. Lokanadham, PGT Maths, such as derivation of Pizza etc. Activity was presented by Master Tarun and Veerendra. In afternoon competitions were organised for juniors and seniors. “Poster Making” for juniors, “Beauty of Maths In Nature” for seniors in M.P Hall at 3pm to 4.30pm.

Date- 22/12/2016

Guest of honor Shree NatrajPatil spoke about “Importance of Maths in daily Life” and also about few mathematicians like Ramanujan, Euclid, Euler etc. in morning assembly. Activity was presented by Master Umesh and Mallikarjun about circles. In the afternoon debate competition was held for juniors “ Numbers are essential for learning for other subjects.” And for seniors importance of Algebra/ Calculus/ Trignometry/Co-ordinate Geometry etc. In other subjects. It was held in M.P Hall at 3pm to 4.30 pm.

Date- 23/12/2016

Our Vice Principal, K.C Maniben spoke few words about “Fun with Maths” in morning assembly. Activity was presented by Anitha and Nalini about game with number. In afternoon mathematical models and Charts were displayed. Origami competition has been organised from 3pm to 4.30pm in MP Hall.

Date- 24/12/2016

Our Principal sir spoked few words about “Importance of Maths In Life” and he explained “Maths is everywhere as God is everywhere” and “Laws of Nature” are mathematical thoughts of God and also explained about “Golden Ratio” in morning assembly. Activity was presented by Anitha and Bhoomika about Area of rectangle is equal to CSA of cylinder. In evening inter house “Quiz Competition” was conducted by using PowerPoint Presentation for both seniors and juniors in M.P Hall.
